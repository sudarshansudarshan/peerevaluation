For Students

Registration/Login

Students can register for the application through the register button, Make sure to use your stuent mail ID for registration.

Once the student has registered they can login, by entering their email and password.

Functionalities

  • Dashboard: The student dashboard displays the courses that have been allotted to the student. Click on a course to view the exam details.
    You can also find No of Courses enrolled, Pending evaluations for you and No of Active exams on the dashboard page.
  • These are the options available to the student on dashboard page:
    1. Course Enrollment: In this section of the dashboard you can look for the courses you have enrolled in detail.
    2. Upload Evaluation: In this section of the dashboard you can upload the evaluation,your answer sheet, for the course and exam taken.
    3. Lecture Evaluation:
      4. In this section of the dashboard you can write on the topic that will appear under the Today's Topic section.
      Your answers will have some intencives.

      Note: We highly advice you to refrain from using kind of AI, as LLMs will detect it and you will be flagged


  • Navigations: You can navigate in your account through this side navbar
  • Profile: Access your profile page to view account details and use the logout feature.

Evaluation:

Here you can see options to perform evaluations:

  1. Evaluate Answer Scripts: In this section you can find about the answer sheets that had been provided to you for evaluation after your exam. You will get option to input marks and remarks based on the questions.
  2. Scores: You can find your score card after the evaluations are done. Here you can see your final score. And don't worry if you are unsatified with the score we will automatically raise ticket and your score will be addressed by the professor twice.
