Professor User Manual

For Professors

Registration/Login

Professors do not need to register. They will receive login credentials from the admin. After receiving the credentials, professors can log in as a professor/teacher for the application.

Functionalities

The peer evaluation application offers the following functionalities for professors:

  • Dashboard: View active batches, enrolled students by batch and course, and manage Teacher Assistants. Hover over buttons for functionality descriptions.
  • Profile: Access account details and log out of the application.
  • Exam Scheduling: Schedule exams only if a subject has been allotted by the admin.

Exam Scheduling

The most important aspect of the application:

  • If no course is allotted by the admin, scheduling is not available.
  • Once a course is allotted, professors can schedule exams by specifying the date, duration, max marks, questions, and evaluation parameters, then clicking Save.

Example of Exam Scheduling Interface

Uploading Evaluations

After scheduling exams, professors can upload answer sheets in bulk using the upload option. Additional functionalities under available batches include:

  • Download all PDFs of answer sheets.
  • Send papers for peer evaluation after uploading evaluation sheets.
  • Mark evaluations as complete once all tickets are resolved.
  • Delete examination data when no longer needed.
  • View analytics and download results via the analytics button.
